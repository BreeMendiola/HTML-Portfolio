The Best Movies According To Bree

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Unicorn Store

A cute and quirky film about a former art student trying to find their way in corporate america after graduation and somehow becoming the soon to be owner of a magical unicorn!

Nope

Aliens on a farm in the middle of nowhere wreaking havoc amongst common folk

The Wind Rises

If you ever need a good cry then this is the film for you
